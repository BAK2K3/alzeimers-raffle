WYSPA

Wyspa Your Secrets, Publicly and Anonymously

WYSPA has been designed to make every voice count. At a time when the world has been confined to their homes, it's important we continue to express ourselves and for everyone to be heard equally.

WYSPA allows you to Wyspa a message out into the world, completely anonymously. Feeling down? Want to get something off your chest? Or do you feel particularly happy, or proud of yourself at the minute? Then express yourself.

Want to listen to other people's Wyspas? Visit the messages tab to select a random Wyspa. This platform works best when we acknowledge we've listened to each other. When you visit a WYSPA, you can click the listen icon to aknowledge you've listened; this in turn makes the Wyspa more visible on the map. Fancy picking someone up? Supporting someone? Or just want to engage with a random stranger in the world? Then feel free to add a comment to a Wyspa.

While you can view Wyspas without making an account, you have to log in to listen to, comment on, and to create your own Wyspa.

Each page has its own help section. If you ever need any more information, just click the help icon again!
